Commissions

If you need a website for yourself or your small business, commission me and I can make it for you!

What I Will Do

  • I'll design websites for any purpose except to promote illegal activity, hateful ideologies, politics, AI, or cryptocurrency. Depending on the purpose of your website, I may request to not be credited.
  • Currently, all websites commissioned will be made with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. More options may become available in the future, though!
  • Please give me an estimated scope of the website you want me to make in advance, such as the amount of subpages you want, what you want them to be for, and any specific functionalities you want on the site.

While Your Work is in Progress

  • Periodically, I'll email you with status updates containing images of the site on both desktop and mobile devices, as well as any questions I may have for you. This is your chance to give me feedback!
  • I'll send you the first status update after about 20 hours' worth of work. From there, status updates will be sent based on what your suggest for the site.
  • If you have any images, videos, or other assets you want me to include on your website, please send them to me. I will not share any assets with third parties, including other commissioners.
  • Feel free to share any site mockups or references with me, too!

Finishing Up

  • Once you're satisfied with the website, you'll have to pay me before I can send you the source code. I'll accept pay through my Venmo account.
  • Because you'll own the website when I'm done with it, I won't host it or pay to do so. However, I can provide you with any necessary instructions and recommendations for no extra cost.
  • Once your site is hosted, please send me a link to it and I'll put it on my Past Work page.

Prices

  • Small Business/Organization Website: $400 initially, $40 per hour of work
