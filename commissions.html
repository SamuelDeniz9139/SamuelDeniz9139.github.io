Commissions

If you need a website for yourself or your small business, commission me and I can make it for you!

Before We Start

  • I'll design websites for any purpose except to promote illegal activity, hateful ideologies, politics, AI, or cryptocurrency. Depending on the purpose of your website, I may request to not be credited.
  • If you already have a website that you want me to update or recreate, I can do that instead of creating a new one.
  • Please share any references or mockups you want the site to emulate, as well as any assets you want me to include. Everything shared with me will be confidential.

While Your Work is in Progress

  • Periodically, I'll email you with status updates containing images of the site on both desktop and mobile devices, as well as any questions I may have for you. This is your chance to give me feedback!
  • The first status update will be sent after about 20 hours' worth of work. From there, status updates will be sent based on what your suggest for the site.

Finishing Up

  • Once you're satisfied with the website, you'll have to pay me before I can send you the source code.
  • You'll own the website once you buy it, and you can host it wherever you want. If you need any help with hosting, I can give you any guidance you need.
  • Once your site is hosted, please send me a link to it and I'll put it on my Past Work page.

  • Small Business/Organization Website: $400 initially, $40 per hour of work
