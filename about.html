About Me

I'm a web designer, front-end developer, and graphic designer based in western New York. I majored in New Media Interactive Development at RIT and graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in 2023. During my college years, I found that I was best at designing and developing websites, and I have continued to refine my skills in both fields ever since. I believe that any company with an online presence should have a functional, accessible, and appealing website. Because the Internet makes it easy for people to interact with companies, a well-made website can give a great first impression. With my skills and desire for the best results possible, I can make sure every first impression your website makes is a great one.
